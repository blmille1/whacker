# SRD 5.2 as the mechanical foundation

Whacker uses the D&D 2024 System Reference Document (SRD 5.2) under CC-BY-4.0 as its ruleset. This was chosen over the older SRD 5.1 (OGL) because CC-BY-4.0 is simpler to comply with (one attribution paragraph, no "Open Game Content" / "Product Identity" distinction), is not viral, and survived the 2023 OGL deauthorization attempt. SRD 5.2 also includes more content than 5.1 — weapon mastery, expanded rules glossary, exploration mechanics, additional species (Goliath), and additional backgrounds (Criminal, Sage, Soldier). All domain terminology in `CONTEXT.md` derives from this ruleset. Future AI-generated content (classes, monsters, adventures beyond the SRD) will be additive layers on top of this foundation, not replacements for it.
